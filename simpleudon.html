Easy Udon Recipe

Description

This Japanese-inspired Udon dish is simple and highly customizable for a quick lunch or dinner!

Ingredients

  • Soy Sauce
  • Mirin
  • Tsuyu Soup Base
  • Water
  • Udon Noodles
  • Green Onion
  • Egg (Optional)
  • Bonito Flakes (Optional)
  • Nori Seaweed (Optional)

Preparation

  1. Cook Udon noodles according to package instruction.
  2. While the noodles are cooking, add soy sauce and mirin to your serving bowl of choice.
  3. Combine Tsuyu with correct portion of water and add it to the bowl.
  4. Mix all the ingredients in the bowl together.
  5. Rinse noodles with cold water after cooking and let drain.
  6. Boil water and add to bowl along with noodles.
  7. Boil and peel an egg (Optional).
  8. Garnish with green onion, bonito flakes, and/or Nori seaweed.
  9. Add any additional desired ingredients and enjoy!
